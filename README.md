### 🗺️ Core Visual Structure / 🗺️ 核心可视化结构

The investigative report is meticulously structured into four thematic chapters, visualized with unique graphics infused with traditional operatic aesthetics:  
该调查报告紧密围绕四大主题板块展开，并利用独树一帜的戏曲元素美学进行数据制图：

1. **Chapter I: Tears and Tribulations (闻者伤心)** Delving into the grueling survival conditions of contemporary opera houses and performers, setting the emotional and socio-economic backdrop.  
   深入探讨当代戏曲剧团与演员的艰难生存现状，奠定情感与社会经济背景。

2. **Chapter II: The Fading Chords (兴衰何况)** A macro-historical data analysis charting the rise and decline of theatrical heritage. **Twenty-one missing characters** are dynamically visualized to symbolize the permanent disappearance of 21 distinct operatic genres.  
   通过宏观历史数据梳理戏曲的兴衰变幻。利用**21个消失的文字**进行动态可视化，象征着21个已经彻底消亡的剧种。

3. **Chapter III: The Disparities of the Stage (兴衰不齐)** A quantitative cross-comparison highlighting systemic inequalities: tracing the paradox of the lowest income brackets among local performers despite their profound cultural contributions.  
   通过量化横向对比凸显结构性差异：挖掘地方戏曲演员在付出巨大文化贡献的同时，却处于“最低收入”层级的现实悖论。

4. **Chapter IV: Echoes from the Cloud (戏台云中)** Analyzing the digital turning point through data analytics and qualitative case studies (including the struggles and modern re-birth of young opera actors like Zhang Junbo and Di Xueli). We uncover how short-video ecosystems are catalyzing a modern renaissance. 
   通过数据分析与案例研究（引入青年演员张军波和狄雪丽的痛苦与新生）剖析数字化转型的转折点。我们揭示了短视频生态如何催化一场现代复兴。

### 🎨 Methodology & Impact / 🎨 研究方法与作品影响力

* **Data-Driven Depth / 数据深度:** Combines rigorous statistical metrics of local opera troupes and income distributions with immersive case studies, bridging macro-trends with human stories.  
  将地方剧团、收入分配等严谨的统计指标与沉浸式案例相结合，在宏观趋势与微观人物故事之间架起桥梁。

* **Avant-Garde Aesthetics / 前卫美学:** The visualization design utilizes a bespoke palette and layout derived directly from traditional opera costumes and facial makeup (Lianpu). It strikes a unique balance between rigorous analysis and cultural gravity.  
  视觉设计彻底告别了千篇一律的常规图表。图表配色与布局直接取材于传统戏曲服饰与脸谱，在严谨的数据分析与深厚的文化分量之间实现了完美的平衡。
